About Me

I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Engineering at Gachon University, with a strong academic interest in Natural Language Processing (NLP). My current focus lies in the design and application of large language models (LLMs), particularly in the areas of prompt engineering, instruction tuning, and controllable text generation.

I am especially interested in how LLMs can be aligned with human intent and applied to solve practical, language-centric problems in the Korean context. I have been exploring lightweight LLM adaptation techniques that balance performance and efficiency, along with methods to improve interpretability and consistency in model outputs.

My goal is to contribute to the development of transparent, reliable, and user-aligned NLP systems that bridge the gap between cutting-edge language models and real-world usability.
