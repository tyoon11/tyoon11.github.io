About Me

I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Computer Engineering at Gachon University, with a strong academic focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP). My current interests center on the design and application of large language models (LLMs), particularly in the areas of prompt engineering, instruction tuning, and controllable text generation.

I am especially drawn to aligning LLMs with human intent and applying them to practical, language-intensive problems in Korean. In pursuit of this, I have been exploring efficient LLM adaptation techniques that maintain a balance between performance and computational cost, as well as methods to improve interpretability and consistency in model behavior.

My long-term goal is to contribute to the development of transparent, reliable, and user-aligned NLP systems that effectively bridge cutting-edge language modeling research with real-world usability.
